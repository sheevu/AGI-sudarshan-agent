The 4-Module Event-Driven Flow

The entire system is a series of serverless functions triggered by specific events. This flow starts with a payment and runs 24/7 to proactively grow the user's business, all managed via WhatsApp.

1. Onboarding & Identity

Trigger: payment.success

  • Function: `onboardUser()` ➔ Creates profile in Firestore.
  • Function: `generateBrandIdentity()` ➔ Calls Gemini for logos & taglines.
  • Function: `saveBrandProfile()` ➔ Saves user's choice to Firestore.
  • Output: Creative Widget 1: "Brand Identity Kit"
↓

2. Asset Generation & UI Automation

Trigger: brandProfile.updated

  • Function: `generateDigitalAssets()` ➔ Creates GMB Profile & Website.
  • Function: `updateStorefront()` ➔ AI "logs in" to user's site.
  • Key API: Gemini 2.5 Computer Use (for UI automation)
↓

3. "Swadeshi" CRM (Reactive Core)

Trigger: whatsapp.message.received

  • Function: `routeIntent()` ➔ Gemini classifies user intent (e.g., 'new_order').
  • Function: `handleSalesQuery()` ➔ Reads Google Sheet & generates voice reply (Gemini TTS).
  • Function: `handleNewOrder()` ➔ Gemini extracts entities & writes to Google Sheet.
↓

4. Proactive Growth (Scheduled Core)

Trigger: cron.daily.8am

  • Function: `runProactiveChecks()` ➔ Scans GMB for reviews & checks calendar for festivals.
  • Function: `sendDailySnapshot()` ➔ Reads Google Sheet, generates chart, & sends 3 AI Action Points.
  • Output: Widgets 3, 4, 5 (Testimonial, Countdown, Snapshot)
